Import your wallet

If you have created a wallet before, or already have a wallet, you can directly click [Import Wallet].


The process of importing the wallet needs to use your mnemonic. It is recommended not to copy and paste directly during the import process, which may lead to the leakage of your mnemonic, so try to enter it manually.


1.Click [Import Wallet], fill in the mnemonic into the input box (please enter a space between each mnemonic) and click OK to proceed to the next step.

2.Set your wallet name, wallet password, and confirm your wallet password according to the prompts, and the software prompts that the import is successful.
