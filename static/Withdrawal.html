Withdrawal


When you want to get your reward back from the node, you can get it in the following ways:


1、Open Block Saver, click [Wallet] to enter the wallet interface.

2、Click [HAH] to enter the HAH asset interface (You can choose according to the required encrypted assets, here we use HAH as the introduction operation)

3、Click [Vote] to enter the node selection interface

4、Select the dops node according to your own vote and click on the information (Node1 is used as the introduction operation below)

5、Click on [Official super node1] to enter the voting details interface

6、View voting node balances or compound interest address balances

7、Select the address to be retrieved, enter the amount to be retrieved, and click [Withdraw]


After successful voting/withdrawal, you can check it in the transaction record, and you can also check the detailed information in the HAH browser through TXID. The node's remaining lock-up period can be viewed in the node's voting details.
