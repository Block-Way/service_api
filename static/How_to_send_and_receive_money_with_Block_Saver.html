How to send and receive money with Block Saver

Transfer and collection is the most basic function of Block Saver. Through the cross-chain bridge of Hash Ahead, we have realized the function of transferring and trading various encrypted assets.


Transfer

1. Open Block Saver, click [Wallet] to enter the wallet interface

2. Click [HAH] to enter the HAH asset interface (You can choose according to the required encrypted assets, here we use HAH as the introduction operation)

3. Click [Withdrawal] to enter the transfer interface

4. Enter the wallet address of the other party and the amount of HAH to be transferred according to the prompts and click [Transfer]

5. After the transfer is successful, you can view the data information of the transaction through the HAH asset interface, or query the detailed records of the transaction on the HAH browser through TXID.


If the transaction on the chain is congested and the transaction cannot be completed immediately, the transaction record will not be opened temporarily.


Collection

1.Open Block Saver, click [Wallet] to enter the wallet interface

2.Click [HAH] to enter the HAH asset interface (You can choose according to the required encrypted assets, here we use HAH as the introduction operation)

3.Click the [Recharge] button to enter the recharge interface of the wallet

4.You can scan the QR code to get the wallet address or copy the wallet address directly to the payer to recharge


Copying the wallet address can also be done directly in the wallet interface.
