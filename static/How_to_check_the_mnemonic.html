How to check the mnemonic

The mnemonic is an essential thing to log in to the wallet software. If you accidentally forget the mnemonic after using the wallet, you can back it up again on the software


1.Open Block Saver and click [Wallet]

2.After entering the wallet page, click [Settings] to enter the setting interface

3.Click [Backup Mnemonic]

4.Click [Start Backup]


Please note that when recording the mnemonic phrase, do not take screenshots to avoid asset loss due to the leakage of the mnemonic phrase.
